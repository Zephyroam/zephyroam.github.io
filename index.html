Biography

Currently, I am a master student in KTH Royal Institute of Technology since 2023.

I obtained my Bachelor’s Degree of Computer Science and Technology from Harbin Institute of Technology in 2023. During my bachelor’s study, my research experience include Automated Machine Learning, Few-shot Learning, Graph Representation Learning and Automatic Speaker Verification.

I am a research assistant (remote) at Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen supervised by Prof. Zhizheng Wu. Before that, I was a research intern at Baidu Research, supervised by Dr. Yaqing Wang, and Prof. Quanming Yao from Tsinghua University. Moreover, I was a research assistant at Massive Data Computing Center of HIT supervised by Prof. Hongzhi Wang.
